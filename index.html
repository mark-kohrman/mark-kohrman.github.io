Software Engineer

Growing up, I wanted to be a linebacker in the NFL. After weighing in at under 100 pounds my freshmen year of high school, I started to think I have the body frame of a fantasy football player. My love of football led me build a fantasy football app called "Fanalyze" for my capstone project at Actualize Coding Bootcamp. My app allows the fantasy football player to create teams and analyze whether they want to start or sit players based on weather projections the app provides. I used Ruby on Rails for the backend, Vue.js for the frontend and two different APIs for live sports and weather data. I'm working on rebuilding the app with a free sports API using Python and Django for the backend so I can take it live via Heroku and make it better (if that's possible). I have many other projects in mind as well.
